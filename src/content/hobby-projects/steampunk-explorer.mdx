## The character

Cheetah from DC's *Bombshells* line — the alternate-universe reimagining of DC's roster as 1940s pinup-style WWII figures. Bombshells Cheetah is the big-game-hunter explorer version: pith helmet, holstered sidearm, cheetah-print accents, and the kind of jaunty pose you only get in pulp adventure illustrations.

## The build

Split between sewn pieces, dyed pieces, and sourced accessories.

- **Hand-made:** necklace, skirt, belt, and sash. The skirt and sash drove the silhouette; the belt and necklace anchored the pinup-explorer detail.
- **Dyed:** shirt and socks. Off-the-shelf fabric did not give the right warm cream tone for the era.
- **Sourced:** pith helmet, holster and assorted gear, red wig, and accessories.
