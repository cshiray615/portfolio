## The brief

A set of event swag for a team all-hands gathering, themed around *The Lord of the Rings*. Two tiers: general swag for every attendee (Lothlórien leaf pin, character stickers) and a special thank-you item for the organizers who helped run the event (the gold One Ring keychain). Plus coins, stickers, and stir sticks designed for ordering, printing, or laser-cutting. The whole set rides on Fellowship-quote callbacks — *you have my sword, deep roots are not reached by the frost, talent is our specialty.*
