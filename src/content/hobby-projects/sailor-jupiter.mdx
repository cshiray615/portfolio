## The character

Sailor Jupiter is the brunette in green from *Sailor Moon*. Tomboy, fights with lightning, can knock anyone out cold. She wears the standard sailor scout uniform with the green-and-pink colorway and a small constellation of accessories the costume reference shows in about three seconds of screen time, which is to say: very little reference for the parts you'd actually have to build.

## Patterns and tutorials

- [Cosmic Coterie's base leotard](https://www.cosmiccoterie.com/base-leotard/) — the foundation underneath the bodice.
- [Green Pepper's Crystal Lake Skating Outfit](https://thegreenpepper.com/products/517-womens-crystal-lake-skating-outfit) — bodice pattern.
- [Sparkle Pipsi's Sailor Bow tutorial](http://sparklepipsi.com/tutorials/SparklePipsi_SailorBowTutorial.pdf) — bow shape, dimensions, and assembly.
