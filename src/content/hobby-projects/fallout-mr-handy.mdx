## The character

Mr. Handy is the floating, multi-armed domestic robot from the *Fallout* series. In *Fallout 3* he's a friendly fixture of pre-war suburbia, still trying to do the dishes 200 years after the bombs fell. The character only works visually if he looks *old*. Crisp factory paint reads as wrong.

## The build

Two parts: the figure and the box.

- **The figure.** 3D-printed from file, then hand-painted. The base paint is fine; the project is in the weathering. Rust on every panel edge, scorching around the thruster, dust collecting in the recessed seams. I aged each pass slightly differently so the wear isn't uniform.
- **The box.** Made from paper and cardboard, designed to mimic the in-universe RobCo packaging look. The figure works as a piece on its own, but the box makes it a *display*.
