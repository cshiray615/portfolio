## The character

The skant is the unisex skirt-trousers uniform that appeared briefly in *Star Trek: The Next Generation*'s first season. Worn by both men and women on screen, then quietly retired. It's a small detail of TNG canon that the cosplay community keeps alive, partly out of love for the show and partly because the gendered uniform debate is, you know, a thing.

This project was actually two skants: operations gold for Casey and command red for Stephen. Same pattern, same construction, two divisions of Starfleet.
