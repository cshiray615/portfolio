## The project

The Vault-Tec bobbleheads in *Fallout* are a series of collectibles: blue jumpsuit, oversized head, white "VAULT-TEC" pedestal. I wanted custom versions with our actual faces, starting with one for Stephen's birthday. Then I made a matching one for myself. Then we wanted them in two sizes.

Scope was the head only. The bodies and pedestals are factory Vault-Tec product. The trick was making a 3D-printed head that, once painted, looks like it shipped from the factory that way.
