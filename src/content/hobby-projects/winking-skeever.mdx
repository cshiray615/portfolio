## The reference

The Winking Skeever is a tavern in Solitude, the capital city in *The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim*. Its sign is one of those small in-game details that has more personality than half the actual quests: a skeever (a giant rat, basically) draped over a circular medallion ringed in Nordic knotwork. I wanted to see if I could pull that off in real wood.

## The build

Four stages, each one slower than the last.
