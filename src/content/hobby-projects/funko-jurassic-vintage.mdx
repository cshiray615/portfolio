## The reference

Kenner's 1993 *Jurassic Park* toy line is its own little universe of nostalgia. Bright coral oranges, terracotta bellies, paint that's a little uneven on purpose. I wanted to take a modern Funko Pop dinosaur and make it look like it had been sitting on a Toys R Us shelf in 1994.

## The build

Airbrushed over the factory Funko. The trick is in the *palette* and the *edge wear*, not in painting on new sculpted detail. Coral orange base, terracotta wash on the belly, the slightest hand-rubbed wear at the high points where a kid would have grabbed it.
